The event will take place at the Met Office Collaboration Building (Met Office HPC Complex) in Exeter. Please note that the Collaboration Building is a 20-minute walk from Met Office HQ (on the opposite side of the M5). See accommodation options below. The Exeter Science Park (and Collaboration Building) are served by the 4A bus service from Exeter city centre. The postcode for directions to the Collaboration Building is EX5 2FS.
